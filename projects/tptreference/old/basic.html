Basic Elements

Walls

[1/10]
Conductive Walls block everything. They can conduct electricity.
[2/10]
E-Walls block everything until they receive electricity.
[3/10]
Detectors generate electricity when a particle is inside. They allow particles to pass through.
[4/10]
Streamlines show how pressure is moving and how strong it is.
[5/10]
Fans generate airflow and give stickmen the ability to shoot air. After placing some, use the line tool Shift+Drag on the fans while having it selected to define the direction and strength of the generated airflow.
[6/10]
Liquid Walls block everything except liquids, Powder Walls block everything except powders, Gas Walls block everything except gases and Energy Walls block everything except energy particles.
[7/10]
Absorbing Walls absorb any particle that touches them, but they allow air to pass through, Air Walls block everything, but allow air to pass through and Airblock Walls block air, but allow particles to pass through.
[8/10]
Conductor Walls conduct electricity and allow all particles to pass through.
[9/10]
E-Holes holds particles and releases them when electrified. They form black holes when too much mass is put into them. Each pixel of E-Hole can have up to 1500 parts in it before it forms a black hole. They will also remove jetpacks from stickmen.
[10/10]
Gravity Walls cancel the effect of newtonian gravity. When a box of it is drawn, newtonian gravity gets cancelled inside the box. They provide stickmen with jetpacks when they touch them, these release plasma and allow stickmen to fly.

Liquids and Solids

[1/8]
LAVA solidifies to molten material under the melting temperature of that material. When it is spawned, it cools into STNE. If you change its ctype, you can come out with any type of LAVA like molten WATR.
[2/8]
BIZR contradicts normal physics, it solidifies when heated and evaporates when cooled. When coloured with deco colors, it will transfer its colour to other elements it touches.
[3/8]
GEL can absorb WATR, which makes it become darker and less viscous and its heat conductivity increases. If in contact with SPNG, WATR passes from GEL to SPNG. When GEL touches PSTE it will drain the WATR from the PSTE and turn it into CLST. If a gas touches the surface, then it will be randomly pulled across it and deposited somewhere else.
[4/8]
SOAP creates bubbles at about +0.50 pressure, the bubbles have the same color as the soap and will freeze at lower temperatures. SOAP can also remove deco colours.
[5/8]
MERC is a liquids that can conduct electricity. It expands when heated and contracts when cooled.
[6/8]
VIRS will turn almost every element into more VIRS. VIRS turns into gas when heated and solid when cooled. If SOAP touches it, it will turn VIRS into what it was before.
[7/8]
FILT is a good heat conductor. Its colour changes depending on its temperature. It can colour BIZR, BRAY and PHOT that pass through it. This element will be discussed in details later.
[8/8]
TTAN conducts electricity. It can block air pressure.
